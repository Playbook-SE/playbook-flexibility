Business Model & Impact

Objective: demonstrate business credibility and viability. HOW value is created.

Revenue streamsto quantify
Revenue sources: subscription to the offer (Exchange subscription key), usage revenue tied to the volume of flexibility activated, additional revenue (AI modules, advanced reporting). Indirect revenue via the aggregator ecosystem.
Cost structureto detail
Main costs: delivery and initial integration, platform run (hosting, monitoring), multi-level support (L1-L3), evolution of the offer (R&D, regulatory compliance).
Value measurementROI in progress
Business value indicators: flexibility revenue generated for the customer, energy savings, value created for SE (ARR, margin, retention). The existing ROI calculator lays the first foundations.
Success stories / references2 references
Concrete cases and pilots illustrating real impact: SDEM50 (public sector, Manche department — 172 transactions, 784 kWh in H1 2026) and IntenCity (SE headquarters, Grenoble — 5 transactions, 450 kWh, 100% confirmed). To be expanded with financial results.
